How to adopt

Your adoption will be finished in 3 simple steps

01

Choose a poppy or a kitten

You can simply choose the poppy or kitten you are interested in on this website. After your finish your form, our staff will contact you in a second.

02

Get your love

You can come to our place place to pick your love. However, if you live far away from our place, we can deliver the cutie to your place for free!

03

Keep in touch

If you are a newbie to keep a pet, You don't need to worry at all! We will give you detailed advice about how to take care of your new family member in your house.
